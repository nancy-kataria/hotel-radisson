About Hotel Raddison

Hotel Raddison was established by Mr. Charles Doe at Colva in 1998. It was a two star Hotel with a minor area. Colva did not have much tourism sights so it was then decided in 2006 to move the hotel location to Bardez Taluka in North Goa. The Hotel gained Four star rating in Bardez Taluka. Now It is a Beach Resort located near Vagator Beach which attracts a lot of tourists. Vagator beach is the northernmost beach of Bardez Taluka, Goa. The hotel is just a kilometer away from the beach and a few waterfalls which makes it the most suitable hotel for accommodation and entertainment.
