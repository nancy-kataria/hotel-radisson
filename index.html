The History Of Our Hotel

Hotel Raddison was established by Mr. Charles Doe at Colva in 1998. It was a two star Hotel with a minor area. Colva did not have much tourism sights so it was then decided in 2006 to move the hotel location to Bardez Taluka in North Goa. The Hotel gained Four star rating.
